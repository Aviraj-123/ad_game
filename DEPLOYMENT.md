## 🎮 HOW TO PLAY

1. **Start Game**: Click PLAY button
2. **Move**: Use WASD or Arrow keys
3. **Attack**: Press Space
4. **Collect**: Walk over coins and gems
5. **Defeat Enemies**: Attack all enemies to progress
6. **Equip Items**: Press I, then click items to equip
7. **Buy Upgrades**: Press S in game to open shop
8. **Pause**: Press P during gameplay
9. **Progress**: Beat all 15 levels to win!
